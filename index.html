Hi-Lo Card Counting

Card counting tracks the ratio of high cards to low cards remaining. A positive count means the deck favors the player!


Values:

  • +1: 2, 3, 4, 5, 6
  • 0: 7, 8, 9
  • -1: 10, J, Q, K, A

Quick Tips:

  • Cancel out: When you see a high card and low card pair, they equal 0! Ignore them simultaneously.
  • True Count: Divide your running count by the rough number of decks left. Play huge on high true counts (+2 and up).
  • Stealth: Practice tracking the count while holding a conversation.
